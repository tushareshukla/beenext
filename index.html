Our Community of Entrepreneurs

A diverse community of builders and believers driven by an innate need to challenge the unknown and create a lasting impact. Our global community empowers founders to cross-pollinate ideas and connect the dots.

Our Journey

2000 - 2013

The concept of a “community” evolves and grows with the founder mindset at its core, inspired by emerging markets and technological innovation.

2014

The very first BEE Global Camp was held in Singapore, a camp with over 30 founders coming together to learn, innovate and bond.

2015

Our focus consolidates with the launch of BEENEXT in the form of our BEENEXT1 Fund valued at USD 80 million.

Hosted the second BEE Global Camp in Indonesia.

2016

Continued forging of cross-border communities in India with BEE Global Camp’s first-ever session hosted in Bengaluru, attended by 122 founders from 58 companies across 12 countries.

2017

Our BEENEXT2 Fund doubles down on investing in pre-seed / seed stage companies across Southeast Asia and India.

2018

Reinforcing our commitment to hyperlocal focus with our Opportunity Fund.

The fourth BEE Global Camp in Ho Chi Minh, Vietnam brings together 155 founders from 133 companies across 17 countries.

2019

The Japanese SaaS Founder Movement kickstarts with the All Star SaaS Fund.

We also interlude in Japan for our first dedicated SaaS conference & BEE Global Camp; attended by 200+ founders from 15 countries.

In addition, our momentum continues with the BEENEXT3 Fund.

2020

We scale up to invest in growth-stage companies with the BEENEXT Accelerate Fund 1.

Our Japanese investment grows with All Star SaaS 2.

BEE Global Camp goes online due to the pandemic and founders forge ties to take the COVID challenge head-on.

Read our blog

2021

With the USD 140 million BEENEXT Asia Fund and All Star SaaS Bridge Fund accelerate innovation.

2022

Our investments expand to include companies with our BEENEXT Asia Fund 2 and All Star SaaS 3 Fund.

Our much-awaited reunion after two years at BEE Global Camp in Bali, Indonesia; attended by 300+ founders from 168 companies across 18 countries.

2023

BEETogether drives regional focus; a micro-gathering of like-minded people in Vietnam, Delhi & Bengaluru.

BEENEXT Accelerate Fund launched to support fast-growing companies from our early-stage startups.

2024

Hosted the second BEETogether in Jakarta where over 70 founders and other ecosystem partners gathered to share their insights and vision for Indonesia’s start-up ecosystem.

Today

We embark on the next phase of our journey as a self-evolving, diverse Community of Founders of over 290 companies across verticals - health tech, climate-tech, new supply chain, deep tech, space tech, fintech & SaaS.
